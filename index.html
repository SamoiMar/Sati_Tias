Advice for your practice management
People who wish to try practice
  • Set the right intention – you do it for your wellbeing: physical and mental
  • Choose a 1 day or 2 days a week to take time for yoga at leading class
  • Try to combine during a week yang and yin yoga sessions
  • As with any learning process (new language, dance, math, car driving) don’t search for immediate results, but give yourself time to enjoy the learning
People who practice regularly
  • Remember about your intention - you do it for your well-being: physical and mental
  • Aside of leading or online classes, set time for regular self-practice – just 10 minutes a day: regular small steps are more valuable than rare big jumps
  • Be opened and attentive to others: listen carefully, speak mindfully
  • Try mindfulness meditation if you haven’t done it yet
People who are keen to teach
  • Take every chance to teach. Learn more by teaching
  • Balance theory and practice – both stretching of body and reflection of mind are parts of your teacher’s identity
  • Share your ideas –they stay alive only if shared
  • Download a Mindfulness meditation session on your smartphone. Use it as instructed meditation 10 or 20 minutes every day
